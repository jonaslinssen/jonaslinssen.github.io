Some Questions

  • Let $T$ be a monad on some category $\mathcal{C}$. Under which conditions on $T$ and $\mathcal{C}$ does the free functor $F:\mathcal{C} \rightarrow T\operatorname{-Alg}$ satisfy IBN (invariant basis number)? In other words, when does it reflect isomorphism type in the sense that $FX \cong FY$ implies $X \cong Y$? Note that we do not ask the isomorphism of algebras to be induced by a morphism of objects, hence the question is different from asking whether $F$ is conservative.
  • Let $T$ be a monad on some category $\mathcal{C}$. When does the category of algebras $T\operatorname{-Alg}$ have the Nielsen-Schreier property in that subalgebras of free algebras are free? A probably weaker question is: When is the essential image of the free functor closed under retracts?
